# Privacy Policy

**Language Bubble**
Last updated: April 7, 2026

## Overview

Language Bubble is a desktop overlay application that displays your current keyboard language near the text cursor. This privacy policy explains what data Language Bubble accesses, how it is used, and your choices regarding your information.

## Data Collection

Language Bubble does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The application operates entirely offline on your device.

Specifically, Language Bubble:

- Does **not** collect any personal information
- Does **not** track usage or analytics
- Does **not** use cookies or similar technologies
- Does **not** send any data over the internet
- Does **not** connect to any external servers
- Does **not** store any user-identifiable information

## Data Accessed Locally

To provide its core functionality, Language Bubble accesses the following data locally on your device. None of this data leaves your computer.

### Keyboard Input

Language Bubble monitors keyboard events solely to detect language switch key presses (CapsLock, Alt+Shift, Win+Space). Keystrokes are not recorded, logged, or stored in any form.

### Keyboard Layout Information

The application reads your installed keyboard layouts and the currently active layout from Windows to display the correct language indicator.

### Text Cursor Position

Language Bubble uses Windows UI Automation and Accessibility APIs to determine the position of your text cursor, so the language indicator can appear near where you are typing. No text content is read or stored.

### User Preferences

Your settings (display mode, size, key bindings, start with Windows) are stored locally in the Windows Registry under `HKEY_CURRENT_USER\Software\LanguageBubble`. These settings remain on your device and are never transmitted.

### System Theme

The application reads your Windows theme setting (light or dark mode) to match its appearance to your system preferences.

## Third-Party Services

Language Bubble does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

Language Bubble does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above and published alongside the application.
